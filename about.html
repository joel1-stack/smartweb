Meet Sephat Makokha

CEO & Lead Accountant

I started Smart Accountants because I saw too many business owners in Kenya working hard but flying blind on their finances. They didn't know if they were profitable. They feared KRA. They were one penalty away from disaster.

After years in corporate finance and tax compliance, I realized the businesses that needed help most couldn't afford Big 4 firms — and online tools left them to figure it out alone.

"Every business owner deserves to know their numbers without needing a finance degree."

That's why we built Smart Accountants around WhatsApp. Forward your receipts. Get your reports. Know your profit. Simple.

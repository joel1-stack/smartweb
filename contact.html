Frequently Asked Questions

What types of businesses do you work with?

+

We work with businesses of all sizes including SMEs, startups, corporates, and organizations across various industries in Kenya. Our services are tailored to meet the specific needs of each client.

How quickly can you start working on our accounting needs?

+

We can typically begin working with you within 1-3 business days after our initial consultation. For urgent matters, we can arrange a quicker start based on your specific requirements.

What accounting software do you work with?

+

We specialize in QuickBooks (both Desktop and Online versions) but also have experience with other accounting software. We can also help you set up and implement the accounting system that best fits your business.

How do you ensure confidentiality of our financial information?

+

We take client confidentiality very seriously. All our staff sign confidentiality agreements, and we have secure systems in place to protect your financial data. We follow strict protocols to ensure your information remains private and secure.

Do you offer customized packages for small businesses?

+

Yes, we offer flexible and affordable packages tailored to small businesses. We understand that every business has unique needs and budget constraints, so we work with you to create a package that provides the services you need at a price that works for you.

Sephat Makokha

CEO, Smart Accountants | Accounting & Finance Professional | Taxation & Compliance Specialist

I am an experienced Accounting and Finance professional and the CEO of Smart Accountants, a firm dedicated to delivering accurate, reliable, and transparent financial solutions to businesses. I have a strong background in financial reporting, taxation, compliance, and strategic financial management.

Over the years, I have prepared financial statements, managed tax returns, streamlined accounting systems, and improved internal controls for multiple organizations. My experience spans corporate finance, payroll, accounts reconciliation, budgeting, cost control, and performance analysis.

I am committed to accountability, integrity, and helping businesses make informed financial decisions. With advanced proficiency in accounting software and a passion for clean financial structures, I ensure that every client receives professional, timely, and compliant financial services.
